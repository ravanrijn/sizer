The Air Jordan 5 Retro: Original comfort, reimagined

The Air Jordan 5 Retro Shoe is a reincarnation of the legendary game-changing design. Renowned Nike designer Tinker Hatfield drew inspiration from the World War II Mustang fighter plane to create this shoe, made with the translucent sole and plush comfort of the original.

Benefits

  • Full-grain leather and durabuck upper for a premium look and feel
  • Inner sleeve for a snug, comfortable fit
  • Rubber outsole with herringbone pattern for traction
